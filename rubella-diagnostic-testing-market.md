<p><p>The Rubella Diagnostic Testing Market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Flow Cytometry** - This technique analyzes the physical and chemical characteristics of particles in a fluid as they pass through a laser.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Latex Slide Agglutination Test** - Uses latex particles coated with antigens or antibodies to detect specific antibodies in a sample visually.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Monoclonal Antibodies** - Specifically designed antibodies used for targeted detection of the rubella virus.</p><p>  </p><p>4. **Immunoassays** - Biochemical tests that measure the presence of antigens or antibodies via antigen-antibody interactions.</p><p>5. **Serological Testing** - Evaluates blood samples for antibodies to determine past infections.</p><p>6. **Clinical Evaluation** - Involves comprehensive assessments of symptoms and medical history for diagnosis.</p></p>
